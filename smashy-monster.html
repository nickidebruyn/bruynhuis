Full Description

Monster bug smasher is a game about smashing monster bugs with your finger on your mobile device screen before they eat your food. Squash the bugs on screen before they reach the bottom of the screen. This casual game will let you smash those bugs in different ways, but be aware, they will eat your food. A variety of monster bugs such as ants, spiders and more. Feel the bugs crunch under your finger as they are smashed.
