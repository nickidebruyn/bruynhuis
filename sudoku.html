Full Description

Test Your Skills and Beat the Clock!


Dive into the ultimate Sudoku experience with hundreds of puzzles across 4 difficulty levels! Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned Sudoku master, there's a challenge waiting for you.


How to play
  1. Choose your preferred difficulty level.
  2. Fill the grid so every row, column, and 3x3 box contains the numbers 1–9.
  3. Avoid mistakes and race against the clock to secure a high score!

Game features
  • 4 Difficulty Levels: Start with Easy and work your way up to Expert!
  • Hundreds of Puzzles: Keep your brain sharp with endless variety.
  • 3 Lives Per Game: Make every move count! Three mistakes, and it's game over.
  • Timed Gameplay: A ticking clock adds a thrilling twist—solve puzzles quickly to maximize your score.
  • Score System: Earn points for every correct tile, but don’t delay—time reduces your score!
